Day 3: Embedded AI & Sensors

Bringing AI to life on small devices!

Tiny Brains, Big Impact: Embedded AI

  • What is Embedded AI?
    • AI on small, low-power devices.
    • Smart gadgets, wearables, IoT sensors.
  • Why it's important:
    • Faster responses (no cloud).
    • Enhanced privacy (data stays on device).
  • Examples: Smart speakers, fitness trackers.

Putting AI to Work: Model Deployment

  • From Training to Device:
    • Get your trained AI model onto Raspberry Pi.
  • Key steps:
    • Model conversion (e.g., TensorFlow Lite).
    • Optimizing for limited resources.
    • Loading and running on device.
  • Software Tools: Specific libraries for deployment.

Sensors: AI's Eyes and Ears

  • What are Sensors? Devices detecting physical input.
  • Types of Sensors for AI:
    • Temperature, light, motion, distance, sound.
    • (e.g., PIR for motion, ultrasonic for distance).
  • Hands-on Activity:
    • Connect simple sensors to Raspberry Pi.
    • Write Python code to read data.
    • Visualize sensor data real-time.
